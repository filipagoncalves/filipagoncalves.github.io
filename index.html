About

A brief story of my personal and professional life. It's all about learning, traveling and being happy.

  • 2008

    FBAUP

    I started my graduation in Graphic Design at Faculty of Fine Arts - University of Porto.

  • MAY 2009

    OFFF 2009 Lisboa

    For 3 days I had the chance to watch and learn from the best! Stefan Sagmeister, Paula Scher, PES and much more.

  • 2008 - 2009

    FEUP

    I got my master's degree in Multimedia Design at Faculty of Engineering - University of Porto.

  • January 2010

    Areal Editores

    I started working at the Multimedia Department in Areal Editores, a publishing company focused on education.

  • June 2010

    OFFF 2010 Paris

    Good experiences must be repeated, this time in the beautiful city of Paris. I got to meet some of my favourite designers and agencies: Non-Format, Multitouch Barcelona, Neville Brody, The Mill...

  • January 2011

    Trip to New York

    If you were to ask me what things I most appreciate in life I would say... Traveling, traveling, traveling :)

  • June 2013

    OFFF 2013 Barcelona

    Here I go again! This time I saw Jessica Walsh, GMUNK, VASAVA, From From... and many more.

  • 2014

    First Surf experience

    I always loved water sports and I practiced swimming from an early age but never had the chance to try surfing. I discovered at 29 what I had been missing!

  • September 2015

    Trip to India

    A lifetime experience. The country where a day has 48 hours and where everything is possible. One must love India.

  • February 2017

    Yeah.. Crossfit got me

    After years of hypertrophy training... yes, I belong in the 1% of the population that wants to get some extra weight... I decided to step out of my confort zone and I haven't regreted it :)

  • ...

    Can't wait to know what future will bring!
